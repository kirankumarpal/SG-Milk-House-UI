About Us


Established in 1997, we started our base by delivering milk. Currently, we provide a wide range of dairy food items such as milk, buttermilk, cheese, butter, biscuits, paneer, shrikhand, yoghurt, etc. Our aim is to meet the growing demands of many dairy products as possible from the local residents. We strive to avail only the best quality and highest customer satisfaction with our dedicated supply and unrivalled service. We only deal with the premium brands and make sure to avail them to our customers.


We provide door to door delivery services of milk & other products.

Frequently Asked Questions

Discover the answers for your questions!!!

1. What is the daily delivery time?

Depending on your area it will be delivered every morning in between 5 am to 8.30 am.

2. What is the last date to pay the bills or shall we pay any fine for late payment?

10 th of the month will be the last date for paying the bill & a fine of 5 /- per day for delaying payment.

3. Is there any advance amount required to pay & what do you charge for delivery?

Yes, you have to pay an advance of 1500 /- & 100 /- per month for delivery.

4. What are the modes of payment?

Online as well as offline.
